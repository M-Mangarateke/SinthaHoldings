Why Sintha Holdings

Sintha Holdings was born out of frustration with an industry where callbacks are rare and accountability is rarer. Founded in 2024 by Sakaria Singo, a PIRB-licensed master plumber, we set out to build a plumbing company that does things differently.

Every member of our crew is trained, licensed, and held to the same standard: fix it right the first time, leave the site cleaner than you found it, and stand behind your work. We handle everything from emergency main-line leaks to bulk water meter installations.

Based in Hebron and serving three provinces, we are a small team with a big reach. Whether it is a residential blocked drain or a municipal bulk meter project, we bring the same precision and professionalism to every job.
